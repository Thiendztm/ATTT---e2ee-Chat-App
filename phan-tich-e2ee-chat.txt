PHAN TICH CHUYEN SAU DE TAI NHOM
XAY DUNG UNG DUNG CHAT MA HOA DAU CUOI (E2EE)
Su dung Diffie-Hellman de trao doi khoa va AES de ma hoa tin nhan
Ngay tao: 30/03/2026

============================================================
1) MUC TIEU DE TAI
============================================================
- Xay dung ung dung chat 1-1 (co the mo rong group chat) ma hoa dau cuoi.
- Server khong doc duoc noi dung tin nhan (chi trung chuyen ciphertext).
- Su dung Diffie-Hellman (DH) de tao khoa chia se giua 2 client.
- Su dung AES de ma hoa/noi dung tin nhan thuc te.
- UI/UX khong la trong tam: chi can giao dien co ban, nut co ban.

Gia tri hoc thuat cua de tai:
- Ket hop duoc ly thuyet mat ma bat doi xung (DH) + doi xung (AES).
- Hieu ban chat confidentiality, integrity, authentication, forward secrecy.
- Minh hoa ro su khac nhau giua "ma hoa tren duong truyen" va "ma hoa dau cuoi".

============================================================
2) PHAM VI DE XUAT (DE LAM TOT TRONG MON HOC)
============================================================
Phien ban MVP (nen lam truoc):
- Chat 1-1 theo thoi gian thuc qua server WebSocket.
- Dang ky/quan ly user co ban (username + mat khau hash).
- Moi client tao cap khoa DH tam thoi cho tung phien chat.
- Tinh shared secret qua DH, dan xuat thanh khoa AES.
- Ma hoa tin nhan bang AES-GCM (khuyen nghi), gui len server.
- Ben nhan giai ma, hien thi plaintext.

Phien ban nang cao (bonus):
- Xac thuc khoa cong khai (chong MITM).
- Co che doi khoa dinh ky (re-key).
- Luu lich su tin nhan da ma hoa.
- Mo rong group chat (pairwise keys hoac sender key).

============================================================
3) MO HINH DE DOA (THREAT MODEL)
============================================================
Tai san can bao ve:
- Noi dung tin nhan.
- Khoa phien (session key).
- Danh tinh nguoi dung.

Doi tuong tan cong:
- Ke nghe len mang (passive eavesdropper).
- Ke chen giua (MITM) trong giai doan trao doi khoa.
- Server doc hai (honest-but-curious): luu/chuyen tiep nhung co y doc du lieu.
- Ke tan cong phat lai (replay).

Muc tieu an toan can dat:
- Confidentiality: chi 2 dau cuoi doc duoc noi dung.
- Integrity + Authenticity: phat hien bi sua tin nhan.
- Replay protection: bo qua goi tin bi gui lai.
- Forward secrecy o muc co ban: lo mat khau sau nay khong giai ma toan bo lich su.

============================================================
4) PHAN TICH LUA CHON THUAT TOAN
============================================================
4.1 Diffie-Hellman (DH) de trao doi khoa
- Chuc nang: tao shared secret tren kenh khong an toan.
- Uu diem: giai quyet bai toan trao doi khoa ban dau.
- Nhuoc diem quan trong:
  + DH thuan tuy KHONG xac thuc danh tinh => de bi MITM.
  + Neu dung tham so yeu (p,g nho, random kem) => bi pha.

Khuyen nghi ky thuat:
- Neu dung Node.js, uu tien ECDH (Curve25519/X25519) thay vi DH co dien.
- Neu de bai bat buoc DH co dien, dung tham so manh, random CSPRNG.

4.2 AES de ma hoa tin nhan
- Chuc nang: ma hoa nhanh, hieu qua cho du lieu thuc te.
- Che do nen dung: AES-256-GCM.
  + Vua ma hoa vua xac thuc (AEAD).
  + Co authentication tag de phat hien sua doi.
- KHONG nen dung: AES-ECB.
- Neu dung AES-CBC thi bat buoc them HMAC rieng, de phuc tap hon.

4.3 Dan xuat khoa (KDF)
- Shared secret tu DH khong nen dung truc tiep lam khoa AES.
- Nen dung HKDF-SHA256 de dan xuat:
  + Khoa ma hoa (enc key)
  + Khoa xac thuc (neu can)
  + Co the sinh them khoa gui/nhan rieng chieu

============================================================
5) THIET KE GIAO THUC DE XUAT (DON GIAN NHUNG DUNG)
============================================================
B0) Khoi tao user
- Moi user co id, tai khoan, mat khau hash (Argon2/bcrypt).
- (Nang cao) Moi user co identity key dai han de ky khoa phien.

B1) Bat dau phien chat A <-> B
- A tao ephemeral DH key pair: (a, g^a mod p) hoac X25519 keypair.
- A gui public key_A cho B qua server.
- B tao ephemeral keypair, gui public key_B lai.
- Ca 2 tinh shared secret S.

B2) Dan xuat khoa
- K_session = HKDF(S, salt, context="chat A-B session t").
- Tach thanh K_send, K_recv theo huong gui/nhan.

B3) Gui tin nhan
- Moi tin nhan:
  + Tao nonce/IV moi (12 bytes cho GCM), KHONG trung lap voi cung khoa.
  + ciphertext, tag = AES-GCM-Encrypt(K_send, nonce, plaintext, AAD)
  + AAD nen chua: senderId, receiverId, messageCounter, timestamp.
- Goi du lieu gui server: {sessionId, senderId, receiverId, nonce, ciphertext, tag, counter}

B4) Nhan tin nhan
- Ben nhan kiem tra counter (chong replay/out-of-order co ban).
- AES-GCM-Decrypt(K_recv, nonce, ciphertext, tag, AAD).
- Neu sai tag => huy ban tin, bao loi integrity.

B5) Ket thuc / doi khoa
- Re-key dinh ky (VD: moi 100 tin hoac 30 phut).
- Xoa khoa khoi RAM khi dong chat/thoat app.

============================================================
6) KIEN TRUC HE THONG
============================================================
Client:
- UI co ban (dang nhap, danh sach ban be, khung chat, nut gui).
- Crypto module (DH/ECDH, HKDF, AES-GCM, nonce/counter).
- Session manager (quan ly khoa theo tung cuoc tro chuyen).

Server:
- Auth + user management.
- Signaling/relay (WebSocket).
- Khong giai ma tin nhan.
- Luu tru metadata toi thieu (ai gui ai, thoi gian, trang thai).

Database:
- User, profile toi thieu.
- (Tuy chon) luu ciphertext thay vi plaintext.

============================================================
7) CAC LOI BAO MAT THUONG GAP (CAN TRANH)
============================================================
1. Dung DH ma khong xac thuc khoa cong khai => MITM.
2. Tai su dung nonce voi AES-GCM => rat nguy hiem, co the lo thong tin.
3. Dung random yeu (Math.random) thay vi CSPRNG.
4. Dung chung 1 khoa qua lau, khong re-key.
5. Luu private key hoac session key dang plaintext tren dia.
6. Khong kiem tra authentication tag khi giai ma.
7. Tin vao timestamp client ma khong co kiem soat replay/counter.
